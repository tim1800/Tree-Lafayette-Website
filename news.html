Arbor Day 2016

Columbian Park was the site for this year's Arbor Day. 201 volunteers planted 71 trees in the rain.

Corporate contributors for Arbor Day were: Tipmont REMC EnviroWatts, Community Foundation of Greater Lafayette, Lowes, DNR/CUF, PFCU, Kirby Risk, and Wabash National Corp.

Neighborhood tree stewards

Neighborhood Tree Stewards help plant trees on Arbor Day and help prune trees with the volunteers of Tree Lafayette. They will represent their neighborhood to help neighbors with their trees. The follow list are tree stewards:

    • Don Staley is the tree steward of Columbian Park neighborhood
    • Rob Pahl is the tree steward of Winding Creek neighborhood
    • Torbert Rocheford is the tree steward of Centennial neighborhood
    • Arlan Stavnheim is the tree steward of Saw Mill Run neighborhood
    • Sam Biederstedt is the tree steward of Jesco Hills neighborhood
    • John Slaven is the tree steward of Orchard Heights neighborhood
    • Rich McDaniels is the tree steward of Wildcat Valley Estates
